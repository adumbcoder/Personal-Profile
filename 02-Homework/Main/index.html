About Me

Hi, I'm Joshua. I started out as a welder. I love to weld and have a stocked garage so I can weld and fabricate whenever I want. I've been welding and fabricating since early 2012.

Around the time I got into welding I was debating on learning to program. Didn't really like the college setting so I went the welding route. I've always loved computers, so now here I am learning full stack development.

I'm a detail oriented thinker and worker. I hate leaving projects unfinished and hate when it's not the best I can do. I'm an honest and hard working person that doesn't quit until the job is done.

My hobbies include reading, gaming, welding, computers, listening to music and learning. I'm a father of three and engaged to a beautiful women.
